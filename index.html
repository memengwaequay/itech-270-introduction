Who am I?

My name is Yvonne Hardy, my spirit name is "Butterfly Woman", my clan is Bullhead. I am Anishinabeg from Leech Lake Band of Ojibwe of Cass Lake, Minnesota. I have four children, one son, three girls and seventeen grandchildren.

Why did I take this class?

I enjoy being creative in sewing, and on computers with graphic design. I hope to pursue a degree in Graphic Design along with Web Page Design.
